Original Coffee

Within our assortment, we offer a comprehensive selection of coffee from various parts of the world. We have carefully chosen both espresso beans and beans suitable for filter coffee to ensure a quality-rich taste experience. We also provide biodegradable coffee capsules, as well as flavor packs containing a blend of different coffee varieties. Explore our range of coffee and find the products that meet your needs.

Self Roasted

"Discover the joy of roasting your own coffee beans with our self-roasting selection. Whether you're a seasoned home roaster or a beginner looking to explore the art of coffee roasting, we offer high-quality green coffee beans sourced from diverse regions around the globe. From the rich, earthy tones of Ethiopian Yirgacheffe to the bright acidity of Colombian beans, our selection provides a wide range of flavor profiles to suit every palate."
